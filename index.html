QUESTIONS & ANSWERS

Here are some answers to common questions. If you need anything else, please reach out to us!

What is the dress code?

The dress code is Cocktail or Formal attire (e.g. Black-Tie Optional). The ceremony and reception will be held indoors, but the grounds offer beautiful spaces to walk, so please choose footwear accordingly.

Are children welcome?

While we love all the children in our lives, our wedding day celebrations will be an adult-only event. We hope you can take the evening to relax, dance, and celebrate with us!

Is parking available at the venue?

Yes, there is a large, complimentary on-site parking lot at the entrance of The Ravenswood. You are welcome to leave your car overnight, but it must be collected by 10:00 AM the following morning.

Can I bring a plus one?

Due to capacity constraints at our venue, we are only able to accommodate the guests listed on your formal wedding invitation. Thank you for understanding.

When should I RSVP by?

Please submit your RSVP form on this website by **February 28th, 2027**. This helps us finalize numbers and meal requests with the venue in a timely fashion.
